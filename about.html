About Us

Mission and Vision

Vision: To uplift and engage underprivileged individuals of any gender, age, caste, background, and creed, and empower them to become valuable and productive members of society

Mission: To foster collaboration and integration with various institutions and governing bodies and develop a sense of compassion through the principle of 'Each One Teach / Help / Feed & Empower One'

Get in Touch

Our Goal

  • Our goal is to create a free education platform and promote the culture of "Each one Teach One - The Power of One." We aim to provide education in moral science, ethics, and mental health, focusing on the well-being of individuals.

  • We believe that education cannot thrive on an empty stomach, an unhealthy body, or a malnourished mind. Therefore, our Each one Teach One initiative is closely linked with the "Each one Feed One" Program.

  • We strive to generate job opportunities for underprivileged women by establishing small-scale industries, with a particular focus on survivors of rape/acid attacks and other underprivileged women

  • We aim to provide computer-enabled online access to education in every village, ensuring that each and every individual has the opportunity to learn.

  • Our organization is committed to providing accommodation and basic necessities for elderly individuals who have been abandoned by society.

  • We aspire to make free and global education accessible to every village and underprivileged area. Our objective is to establish Sahyog Study Centers in each village or underprivileged area, equipped with internet connectivity and computers

  • We will recruit and train volunteers from diverse fields such as history, psychology, science, arts, etc, who will actively drive and educate the local population, with the aim of encouraging each person to educate at least one other individual.

  • Volunteers will be from all area of fields ranging from history, psychology, science, arts etc.

  • Sahyog aims to collaborate with institutions and corporations to acquire resources and additional assistance to achieve our goals.


Each-One Teach-One

  • Organizing seminars on skill development, health awareness, and legal awareness, including awareness campaigns against sexual harassment.

  • Promoting environmental growth and sustainability.

  • Providing classes on demand to orphanages and government schools in remote areas of Bihar
